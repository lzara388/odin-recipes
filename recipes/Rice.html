Mexican Red Rice

Description:

Sometimes referred to as Spanish rice, this simple recipe involes garlic, onion, tomato, bouillon, and of course rice. It can be eaten by itself, or enjoyed as a side dish.

Ingredients:

  • 3 roma tomaotoes
  • 1 tablespoon of bouillon
  • 1/2 of a large yellow onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 cup of long grain white rice

Steps:

  1. Cut tomatoes into quarters, smash garlic cloves, and cut 1/2 onion in half. Add ingredients to a blender along with a 1.5 cups and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour oil into cast iron, turn heat to medium, and add rice to the pan. Toast rice until it begins to brown.
  3. Once rice is toasted, add in the blended mixture along with the bouillon and stir until well combined. Let the mixture come to boil for 3 minutes, them reduce heat to simmer and cover for 30 minutes or until the liquid has completely evaporated and you're finished.
